What Privoraa is for

Built on three promises.

Not thirty features — three things done properly. Everything in Privoraa exists to help you focus, remember, and feel safe.

Promise 01

Focus

A quiet co-pilot for plans, drafts, and summaries. No feeds, no streaks, no notifications fishing for attention — because you're not the product, you're the owner.

Promise 02

Remember

A private, searchable memory of what you tell it — names, dates, the little things. Stored in an encrypted vault on your device. Export or erase any time; deletion is real.

Questions

Fair things to ask.

Privacy claims deserve scrutiny. Here are the straight answers.

Does it really work offline?

Yes. The model and your vault both live on your device, so Privoraa behaves identically with the connection off. An internet connection is an option, never a requirement.

What happens to my conversations?

They're processed on-device and, when worth keeping, sealed into your local encrypted vault. Nothing is uploaded, logged, or used for training — there is no server-side copy to protect, because there is no server-side copy.

Can I use it across devices?

Optional encrypted sync is on the roadmap — end-to-end, keyed by you, so the relay can't read what passes through it. Until then, your vault can be exported and imported manually.

What if I lose my device or my key?

Your recovery phrase restores the vault on a new device. Without it, the vault stays sealed — for everyone, including us. That's not a limitation; that's the point.
